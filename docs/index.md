Hello! I'm a mathematician and PhD candidate at Case Western Reserve University. Currently I'm interested in exploring ideas in double category theory. In particular, I want to understand notions of presentable double categories, compact closed double categories and their use in answering questions in higher category theory and homotopy theory. Beyond this, I am drawn to all ideas that explore the search for truth and the beauty it reveals.
